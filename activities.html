Activities

Facilities are private spaces designed for use by a large quantity of hotel guests.


Hotel Pools

All hotel pools are heated unless stated otherwise. Most facilities will include a leisure pool which promotes recreational aquatic activities. An indoor olympic sized swimming pool. Jacuzzi and sauna. All pools will include dressing rooms for guests.


Gym

All hotels will have some form of gym for strength and cardio training. The front desk will also provide detailed instructions on jogging trails around the hotels.


Spas

Hotel staff will over an extremely hands on spa experience. While the process may be familiar to spa frequents, we also offer a more “hardcore” experience to hotel guests.


Lounge

A large lounge is usually located near the front desk. These places are heated or cooled depending on outside temperatures. Included are choice literature and amenities. All activities are permitted but some public dignity is needed. Try to at least have some pants on please. Unfortunately, these are non smoking areas. Smoking areas are located in properly ventilated areas in and around the hotel.


Conference Rooms

Hotels include conference rooms which come in two varieties. One is the large meeting rooms which include the long table, the other is the open convention room which is perfect for panels.All conference rooms includes video and audio equipment and wifi and internet. Also all large meeting chairs include several restraints and training devices for no particular reason. While painful, these chairs are completely harmless. Also all meeting rooms are soundproof and bulletproof
